## FULL DESCRIPTION (max 4000 chars)

MIND OS is a dark fantasy RPG habit tracker where your real-life discipline builds a real character.

Every task you complete, every habit you keep, every hour of deep work feeds into a living progression system. Your character grows stronger as you do.

?? RPG CHARACTER SYSTEM
Build a hero whose stats reflect your real disciplines.
Train math ? improve Gf (fluid intelligence).
Exercise ? improve Ps (processing speed).
Read ? improve Gc (crystallized knowledge).

?? BOSS BATTLES
Weekly bosses that only die if you show up.
Complete tasks and training sessions to deal damage.
Miss your dailies — take damage instead.

?? SKILL TREE
Unlock passive bonuses by investing skill points.
The more consistent you are, the more powerful you become.

?? MUTATORS
Modifiers that change how XP and rewards are calculated.
Like Jokers in Balatro — they transform your entire run.

?? COGNITIVE METRICS
Track Gf, Gc, Ps, and Um as they develop over time.
See your mind getting sharper through data.

?? POMODORO + CALENDAR
Built-in focus timer and calendar inside the same app.
Your schedule and your character live in one place.

?? RANK PROGRESSION
Climb from Rank F to SSS.
Each rank earned through real consistency, not just time.

Free to play. Premium available for advanced features.
