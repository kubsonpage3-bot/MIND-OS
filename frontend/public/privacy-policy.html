Privacy Policy

RPG Habit Tracker  ·  Last updated: 2026-07-06

This Privacy Policy describes how MIND OS — RPG Habit Tracker (“we”, “our”, or “the app”), developed by Stanislav (MindOs_Dev), collects, uses, and protects your information. By using MIND OS, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account data: username, email address, and hashed password (never plain text)
  • Usage data: tasks, habits, training sessions, XP progress, rank, and in-game statistics
  • Device data: browser type and device type (collected anonymously via Google Analytics)

We do NOT collect:

  • Your real name (a fake username is fully supported)
  • Phone number or physical address
  • Payment or credit card information (PayPal donations are voluntary and processed directly by PayPal)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To save your game progress and sync it across devices
  • To provide, maintain, and improve the app’s functionality
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve user experience via anonymized Google Analytics data
  • To communicate important updates or changes to the service

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored on secure servers (Render.com infrastructure, Neon PostgreSQL database)
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and never stored in plain text
  • All data transmission is protected with HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • We regularly review and update our security practices

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do NOT sell your personal data. We do NOT share personal data with advertisers.

  • Google Analytics: receives anonymized, aggregated usage statistics only
  • Render.com / Neon: our infrastructure providers — they process data only to host the service
  • PayPal: voluntary donations are processed independently by PayPal; we receive no financial or personal data from these transactions

5. Your Rights

  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time via Settings → Reset Account
  • You can request full data deletion by emailing us at kubsonpage3@gmail.com
  • You can use the app with a completely fake username — no real identity is required
  • You can request a copy of the data we hold about you

6. Children’s Privacy

MIND OS is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at kubsonpage3@gmail.com and we will delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify users via an in-app notification or by updating the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
