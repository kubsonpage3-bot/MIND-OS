This Privacy Policy describes how MIND OS — RPG Habit Tracker (“we”, “our”, or “the app”), developed by Stanislav (MindOs_Dev), collects, uses, and protects your information. By using MIND OS, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account data: username, email address, and hashed password (never plain text)
  • Usage data: tasks, habits, training sessions, XP progress, rank, and in-game statistics
  • Device data: browser type and device type (collected anonymously via Google Analytics)
  • Push token: only if you grant notification permissions; revokable in Settings at any time

We do NOT collect:

  • Your real name (a fake username is fully supported)
  • Phone number or physical address
  • Payment or credit card information (PayPal donations processed directly by PayPal)
  • Location data or health data beyond what you manually enter

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To save your game progress and sync it across devices
  • To provide, maintain, and improve the app functionality
  • To analyze usage patterns via anonymized Google Analytics data
  • To send opt-in push notifications (streak reminders, weekly summaries)
  • To communicate important updates or changes to the service

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored on secure servers (Render.com infrastructure, Neon PostgreSQL database)
  • Passwords are hashed using Django PBKDF2 and never stored in plain text
  • All data transmission is protected with HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • We regularly review and update our security practices

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do NOT sell your personal data. We do NOT share personal data with advertisers.

  • Google Analytics: anonymized, aggregated usage statistics only. Opt out in Settings → Privacy.
  • Render.com / Neon: infrastructure providers — data processed only to host the service
  • Cloudflare: CDN and DDoS protection — processes request metadata only
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: push notifications — only if you opt-in
  • PayPal: voluntary donations processed independently; we receive no personal data

5. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are a resident of the EU, UK, California, or other jurisdictions with privacy laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in JSON format
  • Right to Object: Object to analytics processing — disable in Settings → Privacy → Analytics
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for analytics and notifications at any time in Settings

To exercise any rights, use the form below or email kubsonpage3@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days as required by GDPR Article 12.

You can also delete your account directly in-app: Settings → Account → Reset → Nuclear Reset (immediate and permanent).
